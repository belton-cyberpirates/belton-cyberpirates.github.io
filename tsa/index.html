Belton TSA!

We are Belton TSA

The Technology Student Association(TSA) is a program in which students are able to compete with other middle-to-high-schoolers around the world in several different competitions such as Video Game Design, Flight Endurance, Childrens Stories and so much more. Everyone chooses at least three competitions, and then we have around six months to work on them before the State Competition in April. Everyone who places in the top three in any competition will be able to participate in TSA Nationals, where we compete against students from other states and countries that partake in TSA.
